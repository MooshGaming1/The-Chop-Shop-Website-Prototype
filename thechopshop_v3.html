Meet Aarya

Aarya is the founder and sole barber behind The Chop Shop. What started as a small, focused operation in Plainsboro back in 2022 has evolved into a premier grooming destination for the Rutgers community.

After two years of perfecting his craft and building a loyal following in his hometown, Aarya decided to bring his talents directly to campus. He remains committed to that same mission: premium precision, student-friendly pricing, and a sharp cut for every client.
